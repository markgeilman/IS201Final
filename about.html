Great Life, Great Work About My Life

I am the sixth of seven children in my family, with five older brothers and one little sister. Three of my brothers are married, and I have four nephews and one niece. I grew up in Salt Lake County, Utah, and I have studied at Brigham Young University - Provo for two years. My hobbies are too many to count, some of them being woodworking, time with family and friends, and spending time in the great outdoors.

My work experience includes landscape management, home repair specialist, and Resource Coordinator for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I still work at the Church, doing various tasks in the Translation Department, and I also plan to apply for a job at an Intermountain Health hospital or clinic. I have a broad range of skills and experiences that would benefit any employer!
