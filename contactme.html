The World of Me Serious and Fun

I will take every chance I can to learn and grow, both in classes and in jobs. I work hard and find happiness in doing good work. I may not be the smartest, but I sure can work as hard as anyone else. The world can be a serious place, and I often trend towards being outwardly serious, very straightforward and quiet.

However, once you get to know me, you know that I joke around as often as possible! Life's meant to be enjoyed, not just endured. That's why I spend so much time with family, they are the center of my life and the ones I find the most joy with. If you're not careful, I may crack a joke but have such a straight face that you don't realize it. Lighten up, have fun!
